FLOOD RELIEF
GLOBAL TELETHON
2022

This Global telethon is organized to collect donation for flood affectees in Pakistan. This is a difficult time and we need to show support for the flood victims and need to do everything possible for them and give them relief as much as possible. The funds collected through this telethon will be distributed among the flood victims by a committee headed by Insaf Federation.
